# Mémoire court terme — visites récentes

## 2026-06-28 02:45

Well, well, look who decided to crash a deck of cards! This is the Mona Lisa, Leonardo da Vinci's masterpiece, here riding shotgun on the Ace of Spades. Talk about a winning hand — pull this card and you've literally got the Louvre's biggest superstar in your pocket! Now, look closely at that smile. Is she happy? Smug? Plotting something? Nobody knows, and that's exactly the point. Leonardo blurred everything with a technique called sfumato — basically painting with smoke and shadows — so her expression keeps shifting. And here's my favorite trick: the landscape behind her doesn't actually line up on the left and right. Sneaky Leonardo! Oh, and those eyes? Go ahead, take a step. Yep — she's still watching you.
